THE BASIC COMBO

The simplest Lee Sin combo involves using Q (Sonic Wave) to land a skill shot on your target, followed by an auto attack for additional damage. This combo is great for poking and applying quick pressure in lane.

THE INTERMEDIATE COMBO

After hitting your Q, follow up with an auto attack, then use E (Tempest) to deal AoE damage around you. W (Safeguard) can be used immediately after for repositioning or shielding, providing some extra sustain or escape potential.

THE ADVANCED COMBO

For a more advanced approach, use Q to land the initial hit, then auto attack and follow up with E (Tempest). After that, use a Ward Jump with W (Safeguard) to reposition quickly, and finish the combo with R (Dragon's Rage) to kick your enemy away, often into a wall for maximum damage.

THE PRO COMBO

Starts with Flash to instantly close the gap on your target. After that, land Q, follow up with E (Tempest), and quickly perform a Ward Jump using W (Safeguard). Finish the combo with R (Dragon's Rage) to knock the enemy back, often isolating them from their team or securing a kill.
